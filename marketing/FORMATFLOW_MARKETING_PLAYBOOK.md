# FormatFlow Marketing Playbook

## Positioning

FormatFlow Studio helps business teams batch translate PPTX and DOCX files while keeping layouts, terminology and review-ready formatting intact.

## One-line pitch

Translate business documents without rebuilding the format afterwards.

## Primary audience

- Consultants preparing client decks in multiple languages
- Marketing teams localising campaign decks and documents
- Training teams translating onboarding and learning material
- Event teams preparing multilingual sponsor, speaker and attendee documents
- Small agencies handling repeat multilingual document work

## Core problem

Most translation tools focus on text. Business teams still lose time fixing PowerPoint layouts, Word formatting, overflowing text, inconsistent terminology and reviewer confusion.

## FormatFlow promise

FormatFlow turns translation and formatting into one workflow: upload documents, choose target languages, apply controls, export review-ready files.

## Message hierarchy

1. Keep the format: the strongest benefit.
2. Work in batches: saves time on repeated document work.
3. Protect terminology: makes translations feel consistent and professional.
4. Prepare for review: reduces manual QA and client corrections.
5. Built for PPTX and DOCX: clear, specific, and easy to understand.

## Conversion checklist

- Hero says what the product does in one sentence.
- Primary CTA appears above the fold.
- Supported file types are visible: PPTX and DOCX.
- The page explains why layout preservation matters.
- Download section explains Windows installer and checksum.
- Use-case section shows who the product is for.
- Page explains the review workflow, not only translation.
- Avoid vague AI language unless tied to document outcomes.
