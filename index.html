What FormatFlow does

It turns messy multilingual document work into one simple batch process.

Instead of translating one file at a time, copying text back into layouts, fixing broken formatting, and checking every heading manually, FormatFlow combines translation and formatting into a single workflow.

The old way

Manual translation workflows are slow, repetitive, and easy to break.

  • × Upload, translate, download, rename, and repeat.
  • × Copy translated text back into documents by hand.
  • × Fix broken tables, headings, lists, and spacing.

The FormatFlow way

Upload once, choose your languages, set your formatting rules, and export clean translated files.

  • ✓ Process entire batches at once.
  • ✓ Translate while keeping structure intact.
  • ✓ Export documents ready to review or share.
